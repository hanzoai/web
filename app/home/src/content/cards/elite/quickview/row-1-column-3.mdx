**Credit Card with No Limit**

Enjoy unlimited spending possibilities with your Lux Black Card. Every Lux card has the ability to be used for as many purchases as you want as long as the total purchase is not more then 50% of your spending balance.

This means as long as the balance is paid off with rewards, bank transfer, crypto top-up or from yield farming you can continue to spend 50% of it, forever. You must also continue to pay your annual fee.

The annual fee will automatically be subtracted from your available balance.
